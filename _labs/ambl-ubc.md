## Short description

The Applied Musculoskeletal Biomechanics Laboratory studies translational topics that connect mechanistic research on joint health, pain, and motor control with movement and biomechanics-informed treatment options. We work with healthy populations and those living with knee pathologies, including osteoarthritis.
